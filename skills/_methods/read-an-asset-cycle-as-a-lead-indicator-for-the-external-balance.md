# Read an asset cycle as a lead indicator for the external balance

## When to use
When you want an early read on where an economy's external (or any downstream) balance is heading, and household wealth is concentrated in market assets.

## Method
1. Confirm household wealth is held substantially in market assets that swing with a cycle.
2. Establish the mechanism: rising asset wealth lifts consumption and import demand via the wealth effect.
3. Read a sustained asset boom as a widening external deficit ahead.
4. Read an asset bust as a contracting deficit ahead.
5. Use the asset series as a lead indicator over the lagged balance prints.

## Inputs -> Outputs
Inputs: the household asset series; the wealth-to-consumption linkage. Outputs: a forward read on the external balance.

## Failure modes / assumptions
- The wealth effect's strength varies by where wealth sits (the regime caveat applies).
- Offsetting policy can sever the link.

## Worked example
A multi-year equity and housing boom is read as foreshadowing a widening trade deficit before the trade data confirm it.
