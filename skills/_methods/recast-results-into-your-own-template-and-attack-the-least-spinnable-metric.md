## When to use
Reading a results release or any self-reported set of figures from an interested issuer.

## Method
1. Discard the issuer's framing, ordering, and emphasis.
2. Map the figures into your own pre-built standardized template, neutralizing their spin.
3. Compare each line to your prior expectation and compute the variance-to-forecast.
4. Attack the least-spinnable, hardest-to-manipulate metric first (e.g., cash) and let it anchor your read of the softer lines.

## Inputs to Outputs
- Inputs: a results release; your standardized template; your prior forecast.
- Outputs: a spin-neutralized variance-to-forecast read anchored on the hard metric.

## Failure modes / assumptions
- Reading results in the issuer's preferred order and absorbing the spin.
- Anchoring on a soft, manipulable headline metric.

## Worked example (illustrative, single)
Instead of the issuer's adjusted headline, the practitioner starts from cash conversion in their own template and finds the soft beat was accrual-driven.
