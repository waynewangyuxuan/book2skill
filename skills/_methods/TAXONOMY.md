# Method Taxonomy

Unified taxonomy for the atomic-method registry. Each method file's frontmatter `category` equals one of the bucket keys below. Counts are current as of this reconciliation (total: 250 methods).

| Bucket key | What it covers | Count |
|---|---|---|
| competitive-advantage | detect, classify, size and track the decay of structural competitive advantages / moats | 15 |
| competitive-interaction | game-theoretic rivalry, entry/deterrence, price wars, cooperation & surplus division | 28 |
| strategic-position-and-action | framing one's structural position and choosing strategic action / growth | 13 |
| valuation | valuing a business or asset under uncertainty (reproduction cost / EPV / franchise / terminal value) | 11 |
| forecasting-and-probability | base rates, reference classes, scenario construction, calibrated probability, modeling/forecasting discipline | 20 |
| epistemic-hygiene | red-teaming, falsification, hidden assumptions, selection / survivorship bias | 33 |
| causal-and-statistical-hygiene | causal attribution & diagnosis, statistical pitfalls, data quality | 20 |
| analytic-method | decomposition, representation choice, progressive resolution, finding the load-bearing variable | 33 |
| research-process-and-sourcing | edge sourcing, effort allocation, source intelligence & elicitation | 27 |
| behavioral-and-sentiment | sentiment, contrarian timing, behavioral edge | 12 |
| communication-and-integrity | communication & framing, conflict / ethics / integrity | 8 |
| macro-systems | accounting identities & conservation, crisis dynamics, transfer incidence, real-vs-nominal, intervention triage | 30 |

Total: 250 methods.
