## When to use
Before auditing reported figures, to target scrutiny where misreporting is most likely.

## Method
1. Map the structural conditions that create motive to misreport (covenants, targets, compensation triggers, financing needs).
2. Score each reporting party's manipulation pressure from those triggers, before examining content.
3. Concentrate detail work and scrutiny on the parties with the strongest incentives.
4. Treat manipulation as forecastable ex ante from the incentive structure, not merely detectable ex post.

## Inputs to Outputs
- Inputs: reporting parties; their structural incentive triggers.
- Outputs: a manipulation-pressure score that targets scrutiny.

## Failure modes / assumptions
- Auditing everyone equally and missing the high-pressure cases.
- Assuming clean history implies no current incentive.

## Worked example (illustrative, single)
A party approaching a covenant threshold is flagged ex ante as high manipulation-pressure, so its numbers get the deepest scrutiny.
