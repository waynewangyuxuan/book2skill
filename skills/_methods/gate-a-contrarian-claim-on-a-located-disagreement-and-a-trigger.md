## When to use
Before adopting a contrarian view that departs from a consensus of competent people.

## Method
1. When you depart from a strong consensus, assume you are wrong first and earn the divergence.
2. Locate the specific point where the crowd is wrong — not a vague disagreement.
3. Explain why the error persists despite many capable people (what keeps it un-corrected).
4. Name the force or trigger that will make the market converge to your view within your horizon.
5. Without all three — located error, persistence reason, convergence trigger — there is no contrarian position, only an opinion.

## Inputs to Outputs
- Inputs: a contrarian view; the consensus; the persistence and convergence story.
- Outputs: a validated contrarian position or a downgrade to "just an opinion."

## Failure modes / assumptions
- Manufacturing contrarianism without locating the specific error.
- A view with no convergence trigger that can lose indefinitely.

## Worked example (illustrative, single)
A practitioner can name where consensus errs but cannot say why it persists or what corrects it, and correctly declines to treat it as a position.
