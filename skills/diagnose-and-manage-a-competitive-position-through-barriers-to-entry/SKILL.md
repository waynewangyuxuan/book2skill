# Diagnose & manage a competitive position through barriers to entry

A step-by-step analytical **procedure** (a playbook) for competitive-strategy and valuation analysis. Each step names the atomic **methods** to apply — open `../_methods/<name>.md` for how to run each one.

## Spine
Of Porter's five forces only one is decisive: **barriers to entry** = sustainable incumbent competitive advantage. The whole procedure gates on that single test — first establish *whether* a market is protected and *how* (the advantage source, almost always local), then branch: no barrier → operational effectiveness is the only lever; barrier + one dominant firm → defend & exploit; barrier + several equals → manage the interaction (game theory or cooperation); finally express the verdict as value, acquisitions, and growth.

## Procedure

**S1 — Map the arena.** Establish the real market(s) and who competes in each; the barrier test is meaningless without the right boundary.
- Apply: `delineate-a-market-by-competitor-roster-overlap`, `analyze-by-progressive-resolution-of-complexity`, `treat-the-boundary-definition-as-the-most-manipulable-step`
- Gate: start coarse; merge segments sharing a top-firm roster, keep disjoint ones separate; resolve finer **only if** the firm's fate may hinge on it.

**S2 — Test each market for a barrier** (the master branch).
- Apply: `detect-moat-by-two-signal-convergence`, `normalize-profitability-before-comparing`, `estimate-a-mixed-population-from-pure-play-samples`, `distrust-bright-line-numbers-and-precise-coverage-claims`
- Gate: require **two signals to converge** — multi-year market-share stability (≤~2 pt → barrier; ≥~5 pt → none) AND sustained excess ROIC (>~15–25% after-tax over a decade → barrier; ~6–8% → none). Both NO → **S8**. Both YES → S3.

**S3 — Name & size the advantage** (confirming check).
- Apply: `filter-claims-through-a-closed-exhaustive-taxonomy`, `use-reproduction-cost-vs-earnings-gap-as-barrier-detector`, `decompose-stickiness-into-its-three-mechanisms`, `measure-scale-advantage-as-relative-share-within-each-cost-boundary`, `filter-strengths-by-replicability`, `attribute-a-performance-gap-by-reconciling-line-item-deltas`
- Gate: force the source into the closed taxonomy — **supply** / **demand (captivity)** / **economies of scale + captivity** (strongest). No nameable source despite stable share + high ROIC → treat as no-barrier → **S8**. Scale must be measured as *relative share within the boundary where fixed cost stays fixed* (local). Source named → S4.

**S4 — Classify position & interaction structure.**
- Apply: `classify-structural-position-before-committing-capital`, `classify-decisions-by-interaction-dependence`, `assign-a-negative-prior-to-capital-led-entry-without-a-barrier`
- Gate: "ant" outside the barrier / contemplating entry into another's moat → exit, don't enter (deep pockets ≠ moat) → **S8**. Lone dominant incumbent → **S5**. Several roughly-equal advantaged incumbents → **S6**.

**S5 — Defend & exploit a sole advantage; gate growth.**
- Apply: `dominate-bounded-arena-then-expand-contiguously`, `classify-whether-growth-creates-neutral-or-destroys-value`, `flag-over-extension-out-of-protected-core`, `hold-the-constraints-of-a-working-niche-strategy-under-growth-pressure`, `stress-test-a-synergy-or-adjacency-claim`, `project-moat-erosion-as-the-market-grows`, `discount-advantage-by-its-decay-clock`, `watch-volume-and-fixed-cost-absorption-as-the-erosion-signal`
- Gate: **dilemma of growth** — growth creates value only *inside* the advantage; expand only at the contiguous periphery. Market outgrows the niche / scale edge dissipates → **S8**. Reject any synergy/adjacency not backed by a barrier.

**S6 — Select & run the competitive-interaction game** (several equals).
- Apply: `classify-interaction-into-canonical-game`, `pick-a-model-representation-by-whether-move-order-matters`, `solve-a-pricing-rivalry-as-a-payoff-matrix`, `solve-sequential-game-by-backward-induction`, `test-equilibrium-by-stability-of-expectations-and-behavior`, `target-retaliation-by-volume-weighted-cost-asymmetry`, `enter-below-the-retaliation-threshold`, `build-credible-deterrence-from-costly-irreversible-commitments`, `restructure-the-game-to-defuse-competition`
- Gate: **selector = the arena's multi-year behavior.** Price/feature wars (reversible, simultaneous) → prisoner's dilemma / payoff matrix. Expansion ↔ counter-expansion (irreversible, sequential) → entry/preemption / backward induction. Too many players to model → no real barrier → **S8**. Cooperative outcome reachable → also run **S7**.

**S7 — Evaluate the cooperative configuration** (optional, alongside S6).
- Apply: `find-system-frontier-via-single-coordinator-thought-experiment`, `divide-surplus-by-batna-and-fairness-rule`, `screen-cooperation-scope-by-joint-payoff-variance`, `test-an-entitys-survival-by-its-role-in-the-optimum`, `sustain-cooperation-with-paired-punishment-and-return-signal`, `de-escalate-destructive-rivalry-structural-then-tactical`, `partition-the-contested-arena-to-defuse-rivalry`
- Gate: imagine one coordinator → maximize joint pie → divide only surplus above the sum of BATNAs by a fairness rule that survives defection. A firm with no advantage has no role in the optimum. Unfair split → cooperation breaks → revert to **S6**. Tacit/structural means only, never overt collusion.

**S8 — No-barrier / level playing field.**
- Apply: `bet-on-people-and-management-attention-where-no-moat-exists`, `model-focused-attention-as-the-conserved-rate-limiting-resource`, `attribute-performance-structure-vs-execution-by-tempo`, `diagnose-organizational-breakdown-as-structure-strategy-misfit` · *(gap: `drive-operational-effectiveness-to-the-productivity-frontier`)*
- Gate: no barrier → interaction games don't apply; returns trend to cost of capital. Attribute gaps to *management* (distance inside the productivity frontier), protect management attention, set one simple focus.

**S9 — Express the verdict as value, M&A, growth.**
- Apply: `stress-test-a-terminal-value-before-trusting-it`, `classify-whether-growth-creates-neutral-or-destroys-value`, `gate-acquisition-premium-on-cost-savings`, `stress-test-a-synergy-or-adjacency-claim`, `price-any-resource-at-its-opportunity-cost` · *(gap: `value-a-business-in-three-tranches`)*
- Gate: no barrier → EPV should equal asset reproduction cost (ignore growth). Barrier → EPV > assets; the gap is franchise value. Assets > EPV → management is destroying value. M&A: apply the barrier test to the *target's* market; no advantage → pay no premium; only durable synergy is cost savings, premium < discounted cost savings.

**S0 — Wrap (always).** Apply `formulate-strategy-in-three-sequential-goals` → (1) locate yourself re: barriers, (2) if advantaged, manage interaction/cooperation, (3) regardless, set a simple vision + pursue operational excellence.

## When this whole procedure misleads (guardrails)
1. **Boundary manipulation upstream (S1) poisons everything** — wrong market unit → wrong barrier verdict, inherited silently.
2. **Selection-on-outcome / hindsight** — the barriers-only lens can become unfalsifiable.
3. **Treating barriers as static** — advantages decay; "defend the moat" can be wrong mid-erosion (harvest/exit instead).
4. **Single-force tunnel vision** — ignoring 4 of Porter's 5 forces is the source of both the clarity and the blind spot.
